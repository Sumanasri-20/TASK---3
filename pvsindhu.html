INTRODUCTION

Pusarla Venkata Sindhu (born 5 July 1995) is an Indian badminton player


Considered one of India's most successful sportspersons, Sindhu has won medals at various tournaments
such as the Olympics and on the BWF circuit, including a gold at the 2019 World Championships.
She is the first and only Indian to become the badminton world championand only the second individual athlete from India to win two consecutive medals at the Olympic Games.
She rose to a career-high world ranking of no. 2 in April 2017.
